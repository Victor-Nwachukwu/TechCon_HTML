History

TechCon was established in 2011 by two bright individuals who were passionate about technology and wanted to share the knowledge as well as connect with like minds.

Mission

TechCon aims to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower the next generation of tech leaders. We believe in open knowledge, diversity in tech, and sustainable digital development.

Past Speakers

Laura Fin

Laura Fin was a trailblazer in computer programming and developed one of the first compilers. She spoke at TechCon 2014 about the evolution of programming languages.

George West

George shared insights at TechCon 2018 on the open-source movement and the future of collaborative software development.
